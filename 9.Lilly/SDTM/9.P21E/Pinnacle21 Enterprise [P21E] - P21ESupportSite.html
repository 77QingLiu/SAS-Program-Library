 Pinnacle21 Enterprise [P21E] Support Site
Pinnacle21 Enterprise [P21E], used at FDA under the name “DataFit”, provides the biopharmaceutical industry with the key to a fast, efficient drug review process. It’s the technology that drives FDA’s 21st Century Review Initiative, ensuring that submission data is compliant, useful and ready for review — which enables a more efficient review process.
 
Key Features:
  • Measure Your Data Fitness
  • Analyze Data Composition
  • Track and Monitor Changes
  • Manage Data Issues
  • Assess Impact on Review
